This spring, we are making a long time dream a reality: free monthly Saturday workshops throughout the year.

Not only will Saturday workshops help us to support our summer participants throughout the year, we will be able to serve more girls. These workshops will expand the reach of the Octavia Project at a time when programming that empowers young women of color is needed the most.

While funding for our 2017 summer camp has been provided through sponsorship from VanderMeer Creative, we still need the support of our dedicated community members to provide Saturday workshops monthly this spring.


Thank you to our community members who have already pledged their support! Together, we can make 2017 brighter.

Octavia Project is a sponsored project of Fractured Atlas, a non-profit arts service organization. Contributions for the charitable purposes of Octavia Project must be made payable to “Fractured Atlas” only and are tax-deductible to the extent permitted by law.
