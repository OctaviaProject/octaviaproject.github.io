For the past four summers we have provided Brooklyn teens with an empowering community and enriching confidence-building workshops. Our participants put it best:

“I’m very shy, and it’s totally okay to be shy
because the girls at the Octavia Project won’t judge you or criticize you
and are very supportive.”
– Nia, 16


"I always like to test my writing and art on different wavelengths
and see how far I can stretch my creativity. The OP is a good opportunity
for me to do this since we cover such unique topics.”
– Britney, 16


Next summer, with your help, more teens like Aleiyah, Britney, and Nia can benefit from our immersive Summer Institute.
