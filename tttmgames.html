Teens Take the Met Game Lab

The games below were created by teen participants at the May 2017 Teens Take the Met, at the Game Archade + Lab hosted by the Octavia Project, Brooklyn Public Library, and MyLibraryNYC.

Some games were authored by a single writer, some by two or three, or some, as in the case of our collaborative game, by over a dozen. Add your line! Text the next line of any story to @octaviaprojnyc with the hashtag #storyline to continue the story!
