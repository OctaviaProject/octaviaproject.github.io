The Octavia Project is fostering the next generation of diverse women science-fiction and fantasy writers, thinkers, and makers. We provide a free summer program where teenage girls from Brooklyn are given the space to explore science and tech through art and writing, growing their confidence and building skills while dreaming new futures for themselves and their communities. Learn more.

Girls learn skills in science and tech through writing and reading science fiction and fantasy.
Ursula K. Le Guin speaks about the importance of writers who can "see alternatives to how we live now."
Our unique program combines learning in science, tech, art, and writing.
