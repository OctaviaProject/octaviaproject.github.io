Our manifesto

We believe learning happens through joy. We believe that by following your passions, skill-building (and skill sharing!) happens organically. We believe in figuring it out, Do-It-Yourself culture, and learning through experience. We believe that students make the best teachers, and vice versa. We don’t believe in arbitrary lines between artists, writers, makers, and scientists and techies.

Let’s explore art through science, science through art, use technology to daydream, and make the world a more dynamic, hopeful place.

At The Octavia Project, we are interested in how you think the world should be. Through science fiction and fantasy writing, we will create new realities and new futures for ourselves and our communities. Learn more.
